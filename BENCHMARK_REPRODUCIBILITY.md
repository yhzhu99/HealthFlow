# Benchmark Reproducibility

This benchmark rebuild is generated by `scripts/evaluation/rebuild_benchmarks.py`.

## Datasets

- `TJH.csv`: 1,704 rows, 361 patients, split counts {'held_out': 56, 'train': 250, 'tuning': 55}
- `mimic_iv_demo_meds`: 916,166 events, 100 subjects, split counts {'held_out': 10, 'train': 80, 'tuning': 10}

The open MEDS snapshot is checked into `data/mimic_iv_demo_meds/` together with the upstream checksum files and license.

## Canonical files

- `data/medagentboard.jsonl`: 100 tasks with dataset counts {'TJH': 50, 'MIMIC-IV-demo-MEDS': 50}
- `data/ehrflowbench.jsonl`: 100 eval tasks with dataset counts {'TJH': 50, 'MIMIC-IV-demo-MEDS': 50}
- `data/ehrflowbench_train.jsonl`: 10 train tasks

All canonical benchmark rows include:

- deterministic output contracts
- machine-readable `verification_spec`
- `ground_truth_ref` folders under `data/benchmark_ground_truth/`
- dataset provenance metadata

## Paper audit

The original paper pipeline is preserved as provenance only:

- filtered rows: 162
- markdown directories: 160
- selected ids: 118
- extracted tasks: 590
- filter-only rows without markdown: ['122', '123']

The extracted-task audit lives in `data/benchmark_provenance/extracted_task_audit.csv`.

## Scoring

Canonical scoring is deterministic. The evaluator compares required output files against the committed ground-truth artifacts with exact or tolerance-bounded comparisons. LLM-judge scoring is no longer required for EHRFlowBench or MedAgentBoard.
